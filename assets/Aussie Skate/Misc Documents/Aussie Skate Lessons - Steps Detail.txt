Steps to earn the <strong>Basic 1</strong> badge:
<ol>
 	<li>Correct way to fall and stand up on ice</li>
 	<li>Dip in place</li>
 	<li>March forward across the ice</li>
 	<li>Forward 2-foot glide</li>
 	<li>Rocking horse</li>
 	<li>Forward swizzles</li>
 	<li>Backward wiggles</li>
 	<li>Snowplow stop standing still</li>
</ol>

Steps to earn the <strong>Basic 2</strong> badge:
<ol>
 	<li>Dip while moving</li>
 	<li>Forward 2-foot glide on a curve</li>
 	<li>Backward swizzles</li>
 	<li>Backward 2-foot glide</li>
 	<li>Moving snowplow stop</li>
 	<li>Forward alternating half swizzle pumps, in a straight line</li>
 	<li>2 foot turn from forward to backward standing still</li>
 	<li>2 foot hop in place/jump on the spot</li>
</ol>

Steps to earn the <strong>Dance 1</strong> badge:
<ol>
 	<li>FO swing rolls to count of 6</li>
 	<li>Alternating forward progressives to count of 6</li>
 	<li>Description and demo of Killian hold</li>
 	<li>Corner steps in Dutch Waltz</li>
 	<li>Dutch Waltz pattern</li>
</ol>

Steps to earn the <strong>Dance 2</strong> badge:
<ol>
 	<li>Consecutive forward progressive chases sequences</li>
 	<li>Forward slide chases to count of 4 on a circle</li>
 	<li>Consecutive forward swing to rolls to count of 4</li>
 	<li>FO cross rolls</li>
 	<li>Description and demo of reverse Killian position</li>
 	<li>Canasta Tango pattern</li>
</ol>

Steps to earn the <b>Dance 3</b> badge:
<ol>
 	<li>Forward swing rolls to count of 4</li>
 	<li>FI open Mohawk</li>
 	<li>Back swing rolls to a count of 4</li>
 	<li>Backward chases</li>
 	<li>Demonstrate waltz position</li>
</ol>

Steps to earn the <b>Dance 4</b> badge:
<ol>
 	<li>Swing roll with change of edge in relation to an axis</li>
 	<li>Advanced open Mohawk with closed free foot position on exit</li>
 	<li>Cross behind inside chases</li>
 	<li>Rounded end pattern step sequence</li>
 	<li>Two outside edges</li>
 	<li>Demonstrate Killian position and reverse Killian position</li>
</ol>
